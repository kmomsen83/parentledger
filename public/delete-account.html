Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your ParentLedger account and associated data.

How to request deletion

  • Email us at: support@parentledgerinfo.com
  • Include your phone number used to sign up
  • We will process your request within 7 days

What gets deleted

  • Your account information
  • All associated child and case data

Data retention

Some data may be retained if required for legal or compliance purposes.
