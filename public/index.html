Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026

ParentLedger ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Child Information: Information entered by users such as names, ages, and related custody details
  • Communication Data: Messages and interactions between co-parents within the app
  • Usage Data: App activity, device information, and performance data

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide co-parenting tools and features
  • Enable communication and coordination between authorized users
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Maintain account security and prevent unauthorized access

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information only in the following cases:

  • With authorized users within your case (e.g., co-parents)
  • With trusted service providers such as Firebase (Google) and RevenueCat for app functionality
  • If required by law or legal process

Subscriptions & Payments

Subscriptions are processed through Google Play and Apple App Store. We do not store your payment information.

Children’s Privacy

ParentLedger may store information about children as provided by parents or legal guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services.

Your Rights

You may request access, updates, or deletion of your data at any time.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.
